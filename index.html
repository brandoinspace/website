A Little About Me :)

I am currently a high school student who wants to pursue a career in Aerospace Engineering or Astrophysics. My dream job would be to work for NASA working on rockets as an engineer or on software as a developer.

I have a lot of experience in development in languages such as Python, Rust, Java, and a slowly gaining experience with C#. I am also well-versed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have dabbled in C and C++ and I am interested in learning more about both.

I have taken engineering classes in my high school and have experience in Autodesk Inventor and Fusion 360. I also have basic miscellaneous engineering knowledge from the classes (e.g. trusses, gears, forces, mechanical advantage) and lots of math experience.

Why Does This Blog Exist?

To put simply: to document the things that I do as a kind of portfolio.

If you want more of an explanation, here we go! I was inspired to make this blog to document various engineering-related projects (as well as anything I want to write about, even if it is not related to engineering!) as I work on them.

This can be helpful portfolio for me when I apply for internships, apprenticeships, or jobs! If you're reading this because you saw this on an application for one of those, hello! (please hire me :D)
