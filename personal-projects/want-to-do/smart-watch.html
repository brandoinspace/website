A DIY Smart Watch???

I really want to make my own Smart Watch! Well, not a "Smart" Watch, more of just a Digital Watch.

I just want it to tell time without all the unneeded things like heart rate monitoring, fitness tracking etc.

Although I can just buy a normal analog watch, I think it would be cool to try and make a cheap digital one to try out Arduinos and breadboards.

Just a thought :)
